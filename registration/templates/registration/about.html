About Swivly

Your trusted platform for accommodations and student marketplaces.

Who We Are

Swivly is a platform built to simplify student life. Whether you're looking for a place to stay, buying or selling items, or connecting with others in your community, Swivly is here to make it easier, safer, and more transparent.

Our Mission

To empower students by providing an all-in-one platform that fosters trust, transparency, and convenience in managing their daily needs.

Our Vision

We envision a world where students can seamlessly access trusted services and resources within their communities.
